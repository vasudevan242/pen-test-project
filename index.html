About

PENTEST AI is an advanced web application that empowers cybersecurity teams to manage end-to-end penetration testing projects with enhanced efficiency, scalability, and clarity. At its core, it integrates AI-driven capabilities to automate and streamline the repetitive and documentation-heavy aspects of security assessments—while offering a robust project and customer management system.

It bridges the gap between penetration testing and intelligent automation. By embedding AI deeply into documentation, analysis, and reporting workflows, it frees up testers to focus on technical assessment while maintaining rigor and professionalism in client deliverables. From onboarding to final report, every step is guided, managed, and enhanced within one cohesive platform.
